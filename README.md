# neon-overdrive
🌐 NEON OVERDRIVE 
Survive the Grid. Outrun the System.

Drop into Neon Overdrive , a lightning-fast, high-octane retro-shooter built entirely in your browser. No downloads. No installs. Just pure, unadulterated arcade action.

You are trapped in a shifting, procedurally generated cyberpunk labyrinth. The System has detected your presence, and its red-glitch hunter-killer drones are zeroing in on your location. How long can you survive the overdrive?

🔥 KEY FEATURES:

Infinite Arenas: No two runs are the same. The procedural city generator creates a massive, unpredictable neon maze every single time you boot up.

Fluid Physics: Master your momentum. Slide along walls, jump gaps, and use gravity to outmaneuver the swarm.

Dual-Weapon Arsenal: Swap on the fly. Lay down suppressive fire with the precision Assault Rifle, or shred close-quarters threats with the heavy Riot Shotgun.

Relentless AI: The drones don't just wander—they hunt. Break their line of sight, or prepare for a high-speed chase.

Instant Action: Built on a custom lightweight web engine. Click a link, lock your mouse, and you are instantly in the firefight.
